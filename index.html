Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AnimateItNow

AnimateItNow is an open-source platform for sharing and discovering web UI animation components and templates.
How can I contribute?
You can contribute by submitting your own animation templates or improving existing ones. Check our CONTRIBUTING.md for details.
What technologies are used?
AnimateItNow uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Some templates may use external libraries for advanced effects.
How do I add a new animation?
Fork the repository, add your animation template, and submit a pull request. Our team will review and merge your contribution.
